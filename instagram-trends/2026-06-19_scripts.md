The source's key warning — "your goal needs actual numbers so Claude can grade itself" — is the structural anchor. This script adds the ceiling (max passes) as the critical missing piece that the source implies but doesn't make explicit.

**On-screen hook text:** YOUR LOOP HAS NO OFF SWITCH

**Hold line:** "A loop without a max-attempt limit will hit the goal, miss slightly, try again, get close, try again — burning tokens until something external breaks it."
